Nomi Health and 585 Strategies

Working at Nomi Health and 585 Strategies has been an amazing experience. I've had the opportunity to help the world in the fight against COVID-19. To clear things up, 585 Strategies was an operations company contracted by Nomi Health. In May 2021, the two companies merged and now the two are collectively known as Nomi Health. One reason I love Nomi Health so much is because it has a mission statement I can really connect with, "Bringing Health Care to the people". Another purpose of this company is to take the middle man out of health care. I have been a part of that by helping coordinate various mobile site tours where our staff would administer COVID-19 vaccines as well as PCR and antigen rapiad tests. All of what we do is free to the people, not even insurance is required.

Nomi Health Megaplex Site, taking the middle man out of Health Care

Working at Nomi Health has helped me see just how much potential we all have for change. When people come together, there aren't many obstacles that can't be passed. These months with Nomi Health have helped me to develop leadership qualities, a team mindset, and many organizational skills.

Robert Half International

Robert Half International gave me my first taste of what a real professional work environment is like. I learned a lot about professionalism, how to interact with others, and how to stay focused on the tasks I needed to complete. I didn't have anyone constantly supervising me, so it pushed me to be more self driven and responsible. I had a lot of opportunities to use my mathematics skills as well as my memory to complete tasks at an accelerated rate. I will forever be grateful for my time there.

Studio Movie Grill

Studio Movie Grill is a mix between a movive theater and a dine in restaurant. I learned a lot about people skills and how to encourage coworkers. There were many days where a lot of us would work long hours, and those days were the ones that I learned the most. Because of my job there, I learned a lot about working under pressure, while stressed, and with customers and coworkers with bad attitudes. These skills have helped, and will contiue to help me with all of my future professional endeavors.
